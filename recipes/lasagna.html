Lasagna

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: it’s make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients:
  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasgna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How to Make it
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipie intructions
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving
